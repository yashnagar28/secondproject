Jai Shree Gajanan

Shree Shipramukhi Gajanan At Narsinghat Ujjain Madhya Pradesh

Vishesh Sringar Darshan

Jai Gajanan Ganeshji temple is in Narsinghat of Ujjain and in front of ganeshji temple is Shipra river that is why this is called Shri Shipramukhi Ganesh...All wishes are fullfilled by merely seeing the idol of Lord Ganesha and no one ever goes empty handed from God's court...
